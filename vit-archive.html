Upload Guidelines

Please read before contributing to ensure quality and safety for everyone.

What You Should Upload

CAT, FAT, PAT papers you have personally appeared for
Memory-based question reconstructions from recent exams
VITCOLAB assignment questions (screenshots or typed)
Papers shared with you by classmates with their consent
Scanned or photographed papers — quality doesn't need to be perfect

What You Should NOT Upload

Papers obtained through unauthorized access to faculty systems
Content that violates VIT's academic integrity policies
Copyrighted textbook pages or paid study material
Personal information of faculty or other students
Deliberately incorrect or fabricated question papers

Naming & Metadata Standards

Always fill course code, exam type, year, and slot
Double-check course codes against VTOP before submitting
If unsure of exact date, provide approximate month and year
Use the notes field to mention if paper is incomplete or memory-based

Legal & Ethical Commitments

We do not scrape or automate on VTOP in any way
No VIT credentials are stored — ever
This platform is fully non-commercial and open source
All access is free — no gating or monetization

FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about VITArchive.

Is VITArchive officially affiliated with VIT University?
No. VITArchive is a completely independent, student-run initiative. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially associated with VIT University. All content is contributed by students, for students.
Do I need to login to view or download papers?
No login is required to browse or download any paper. All content is freely accessible to everyone. Login is only optional — for personalization features like seeing your enrolled courses first, or tracking your contribution history.
Is it safe to upload papers here? Will I get into trouble?
Uploading papers you personally appeared for is generally considered safe. We recommend avoiding papers obtained through unauthorized means. Our platform is designed for sharing legally obtainable academic material. Anonymous uploads are also supported if you prefer privacy.
How are memory-based questions verified?
Memory-based questions are clearly labeled as such. Multiple students often submit similar questions independently, and when they match, confidence in accuracy increases. Reviewers monitor these submissions and flag inconsistencies. We always recommend treating memory-based content as approximate.
What happens to duplicate papers?
The system automatically checks for duplicates using metadata comparison and file hashing. If a potential duplicate is detected, it's flagged for reviewer intervention. The best quality version is kept, and the contributor of the duplicate is still credited.
How do I earn points and badges?
Points are earned by uploading verified papers, contributing memory questions, fulfilling paper requests, and helping with moderation. Badges are awarded for milestones like "First Upload," "10 Papers Streak," "Top Contributor of the Month," and more. Points never restrict access — they're purely for recognition.
Will this platform survive after current students graduate?
Yes! This is the core purpose of VITArchive — to be a persistent academic memory for VIT that outlives individual batches. The platform is open source, and school/department representatives ensure continued maintenance as student batches transition.
I found an error or incorrect paper — what should I do?
Every paper and question has a "Report Issue" button. Use it to flag errors, incorrect metadata, or quality issues. Our reviewer team will address flagged content within 48 hours.

About VITArchive

The story behind the project.

Our Mission

VITArchive is a student-built, student-maintained academic memory for VIT University. We believe every student should have fair access to past papers and questions — not just those in the right WhatsApp group. Our platform is, and always will be, completely free, open-source, and community-driven.

Our Values
Open Access
No login required. No paywalls. No restricted tiers. All papers, all courses, always free.
Privacy First
We never store credentials. Login is optional and only for personalization, never for access.
Community Driven
Built by students, maintained by students, governed by the community — not any single person.
Long Term
Designed to outlive individual batches and serve VIT students for years to come.
